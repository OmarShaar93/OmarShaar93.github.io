This is done locally and does not involve storing or transmitting any user data outside of the user's device. ### User Activity - The extension may monitor user interactions (such as clicks and keyboard shortcuts) solely for the purpose of providing its functionality. This data is not stored or shared. ## How We Use Your Data The Speed Reading Highlighter extension uses the accessed content and user activity solely to: - Highlight selected text for speed reading practice. - Provide a customizable reading experience based on user preferences. ## Data Sharing We do not share any user data with third parties, nor do we sell or transfer any collected data. ## User Rights Since we do not collect personal data, there are no rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of personal information.
